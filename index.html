Our Vision of Care

St. Leo's Hospital was born from the vision of its founder, the late Dr. Chike Akunyili, to serve humanity with Serene, Excellent, and Quality healthcare. This founding principle remains our guiding light today.

We are committed to being the most trusted partner for healthcare in Enugu. Our mission is to combine medical expertise with genuine compassion, ensuring every patient feels valued, respected, and confident in the care they receive. We strive to create a healthier community, one patient at a time.
